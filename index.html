ABOUT GALAB DHUNGANA

Galab Dhungana is a Corporate and Personal Immigration Attorney with 25 years of experience counseling and representing individuals and corporate clients, including their global divisions, on U.S. corporate immigration, business and work visas, and general immigration matters.

He received a law degree from the University of Chicago Law School and completed International Development Studies at the University of Oslo, Norway. His academic background also includes a B Com, LLB, and an MA in Economics from Tribhuvan University in Kathmandu, Nepal. With a strong foundation in both law and international development, he provides strategic and tailored immigration solutions to clients worldwide.
